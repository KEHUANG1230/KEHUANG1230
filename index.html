Goofy horror game kinda

As you were driving your car, you got into a accident making you lose conscious, few hours later you wake up into a broken down house exploring the house and surrounding you found out that there is a monster lurking around protecting his treasures,

  1. Collect the keys to advance to the next floor
  2. Collect the note to learn about the monsters life
  3. Collect the hammer at the third floor of the house which is his treasure, this can allow you to escape
If on a tablet or phone, simply rotating the device in order to collect the items. If on a desktop, use the mouse and arrows keys to collect the items.
