Steal the Banana and escape

you randomly stumble upon a house, in the house rumored to contain a banana with a moose like creature guarding it. So steal the mooses banana and run away before he catches you,

  1. Collect the keys to advance to the next floor
  2. Collect the note to get hints about the mooses life
  3. Collect the banana at the third floor of the house
If on a tablet or phone, simply rotating the device in order to collect the items. If on a desktop, use the mouse and arrows keys to collect the items.
